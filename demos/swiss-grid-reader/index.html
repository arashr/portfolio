Swiss grid · reader demos

Isolated layout experiments on the reader poster feed. Ground colors and display fonts still come from production assignment (groundForSlug, titleFaceForIndex). Poster width caps and stagger rules are disabled in these demos.

01 · Modular grid

Strict 12-column snap — wide, narrow, and full-bleed blocks on a repeating modular cycle.

02 · Asymmetric rhythm

Left-axis and hang-right spans with generous vertical whitespace between posters.

03 · Full bleed + content position

Edge-to-edge sections, inner content grid, header picks up each section’s ground and display font on scroll.
